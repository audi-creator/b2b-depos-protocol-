B2B Depositions Protocol Navigator

Click through the scenarios to see required actions

Is the attorney staying in the same Zoom room for multiple jobs?
Required Actions:
  • Stop your OBS recording at the end of the first deposition
  • Start a new OBS recording for the second job (before completing pre-depo workflow)
  • Add a note in BOTH Video Worksheets documenting that the same Zoom room was used
Standard Protocol:
  • Proceed with separate Zoom rooms as preferred
  • Each job gets its own OBS recording
  • Complete all pre-depo steps for each job
Did the Taking Attorney change the order of witnesses?
Required Actions:
  • Document the change in your Video Worksheet
  • Notify #video-help immediately so they're aware of the adjustment
Standard Protocol:
  • Proceed with witnesses in the originally scheduled order
  • No additional documentation needed
Did the previous deposition run late, affecting start time?
Required Actions:
  • Open the Review Job Information tab in your Video Worksheet
  • Change the "Start Time" to reflect the actual new start time
  • In Additional Notes, explain which job caused the delay and provide context
  • Alert #video-help if the timing change is significant
Standard Protocol:
  • Maintain scheduled start times
  • No timing adjustments needed in worksheet
Did counsel request exhibits from a previous deposition in the case?
Required Actions:
  • Go to My Jobs page → Completed or In Progress tab
  • Find the relevant deposition
  • Scroll to Job Files section and download the exhibits
Important Note:

If you were NOT the videographer on that job, reach out to #video-help for assistance retrieving the exhibits.

Standard Protocol:
  • No exhibit retrieval needed
  • Proceed with standard deposition workflow
Did you accidentally check into the wrong job during a B2B?
Immediate Action Required:
  • Immediately report to #video-help
  • Check into the correct job to avoid a "late check-in"
Great!
  • You're checked into the correct job
  • Continue with standard workflow
